Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse or search for products, add them to your basket, and proceed to checkout. We accept all major cards via Stripe.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also view orders in My Orders.

Can I return an item?

Yes. Most items can be returned within 14 days. See our Returns Policy for full details.

How do I become a seller?

Click Start Selling to create a vendor account. It's free and takes just a few minutes.

When do sellers get paid?

Sellers receive payouts after orders are confirmed delivered. Payouts are processed weekly.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment provider. We never store card details.
