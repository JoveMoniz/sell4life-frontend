About Sell4Life

Sell4Life is an independent marketplace for buying and selling across all categories — electronics, fashion, books, toys, home goods, and more.

Our mission is to make it easy for independent sellers to reach buyers, and for buyers to discover quality products from people who care about what they sell.

For Buyers

Browse thousands of products from independent sellers. Every order is protected and every seller is vetted before they can list.

For Sellers

Start selling for free. List your products, set your prices, and we'll handle payments. Payouts are sent directly to your bank.
